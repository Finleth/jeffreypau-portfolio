About

Web development has become as much of a part of me as my own two hands. I'm currently a student at LearningFuze, and have been discovering day after day all the excitement and joy one can have while programming. I'm looking forward to seeing where I can take all of this knowledge and apply it to more than just my career.

Outside of programming, I also love music. I listen to classical, metal, and everything in between. I like to play both the guitar and the bass guitar. But I'm first and foremost a singer.

I also love to eat ramen, pizza, and tacos. If I'm not programming or singing, I'm likely eating.
